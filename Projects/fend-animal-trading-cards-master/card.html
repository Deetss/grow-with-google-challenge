Kangaroo

Contrary to popular belief a kangaroo walks around mostly on its hands, only hopping when it needs to travel fast.

  • Scientific Name: Macropus
  • Average Height: 6 ft 7 in,
  • Average Lifespan: 6 years
  • Diet: Grasses and Shrubs

A large hopping animal with an extremely powerful tail and razor sharp claws on its feet. This animal can reach a top speed of 43 mph and jump over 35 feet in a singe leap.
